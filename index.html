Joe Anthony.
Cocktail Maker +
Visual Storyteller

Joe Anthony is a Full Time Bartender based out of San Antonio, TX. He works as a avid recipe devolper and helps run a very quaint bar back home.


In 2018 his journey started as waiter who quickly became bored and wanted more, picked up bartending in 2021 and hasn't looked back. Now he is Head Bartender at Barbaro part of Empty Stomach Group.

Thanks for checking out Joe Anthony's tips and tricks of the trade!
